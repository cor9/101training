How the Program Works

This is not beginner / intermediate / advanced labeling. It is a complete actor-development system. Each offering trains a different essential skill, and serious actors are encouraged to complete all three.

Where to Start

Actors may begin their training in either of the foundational pillars based on their immediate needs.

  • ◆ Audition Mastery
  • ◆ Character Lab

The Eligibility Rule

Master Methods is our most selective and advanced offering, requiring foundational skills.

Required for Master Methods: To enroll in Master Methods, actors must first complete either Audition Mastery or Character Lab.

Serious actors don’t stop at one. They complete the system.

The Three Core Pillars

Every offering serves a distinct, vital purpose in an actor's journey from learning to booking.

Execution

Audition Mastery

"Where are we right now?"

You leave knowing exactly what your child needs to fix—and what to do next.

The Assessment Engine for actors who are auditioning now.

  • Helps actors perform better under pressure
  • Improves self-tapes, callbacks, choices, and strategy
  • Includes evaluation through homework self-tapes and a mock series regular callback final
  • Includes a private 1-on-1 parent career strategy meeting / report card at the end
Outcome

Clarity, stronger auditions, and strategic direction

Identity

Character Lab

This is where actors stop blending in and start being cast.

"Why would casting choose you?"

If your child is “good”… but nothing is happening after auditions—this is usually the missing piece.

The Identity Engine that helps actors stop blending in.

  • Focuses on type, specificity, bold choices, and on-camera presence
  • Helps actors discover what makes them uniquely castable
  • Includes 4 custom mini demo reel clips based on the actor’s strengths/type discovered during the course
  • Emphasizes the creation of tangible, usable casting assets
Outcome

Differentiation, type clarity, and usable casting assets

✓ This class qualifies actors for Master Methods.
View Character Lab
Technique

Master Methods

"How do great actors actually work?"

The Craft Engine designed for actors 12+ ready to deepen process.

  • Teaches foundational acting approaches in an age-appropriate, practical way
  • Touches techniques inspired by Stanislavski, Meisner, Adler, Strasberg, Chekhov, Mamet / Practical Aesthetics
  • Includes 2 contrasting custom polished self-tape demo reel scenes (one dramatic prestige, one comedic multi-cam)

Actors leave with two custom scenes designed to actually be used—on their reel, profiles, and submissions.

This is not a beginner class. It’s for actors ready to train seriously.

🔒 Eligibility required: actors must complete Audition Mastery or Character Lab before enrolling.
Outcome

Technique, process, and polished performance assets
